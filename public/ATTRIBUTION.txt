Globe imagery
==============

NASA Visible Earth / Earth Observatory, Blue Marble global true-color mosaic.
The WebP asset in this application is an optimized 4096 × 2048 derivative of
the world_true_color.tif image used by the legacy GlobeFig scripts.

Blue Marble collection:
https://visibleearth.nasa.gov/collection/1484/blue-marble

NASA image and media usage guidelines:
https://www.nasa.gov/nasa-brand-center/images-and-media/
